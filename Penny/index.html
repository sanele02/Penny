About

Penny is a digital tipping platform that empowers South African retail workers by enabling customers to tip instantly via QR codes, EFT, or airtime.

We partner with local stores to create a fairer system where great service is rewarded helping staff earn extra income while giving shoppers an easy way to show appreciation.

Every tip makes a difference.
